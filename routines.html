Welcome! How to Use This Guidebook

This interactive guidebook is designed to help you achieve multiple fitness goals through short, frequent training sessions. Here's how to use it:

  • Daily Checklist: Use the weekly schedule to check off completed workouts.
  • Detailed Guides: Expand each section to find specific instructions for every exercise.
  • Filter by Day: Use the day buttons to show only activities for a specific day.
  • Progress Tracking: Record your weekly progress in the tracker at the bottom.
  • Search: Use the search bar to quickly find specific exercises or instructions.
  • Dark Mode: Toggle the moon icon for light/dark mode.
  • Print Version: Click the Print button for a printer-friendly version.

Your progress is automatically saved in your browser. Remember that consistency is key - even short sessions add up to significant progress over time!
